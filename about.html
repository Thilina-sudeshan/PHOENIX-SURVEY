About Us

Quick Transport and Logistics Solutions

Phoenix Cargo Controllers & Claims Adjusters are Marine Cargo Inspectors active in inspection of various export and import cargoes of today's trade & commerce.

We are a fully owned Indian company. For over 20 years we have gained confidence of different clients, this despite the competition of International Giants in the field of inspection.

Our exact independent reporting has resulted, at times in the rejection of those cargoes not meeting the contractual requirements. This has influenced many traders to consider our service on an exclusive basis.

Our Features

Trusted Marine & Cargo Surveyors

Enormous risks are involved with the purchase of commodities, where Quality & Quantity are the ultimate factors. The real test of an effective survey company at the port of loading would be minimal claims and complaints at the time of delivery of consignments at the destination.

With a qualified staff of around 45 experts in commodity handling and modern equipment, we ensure the highest standards of service in Marine & Cargo surveys.
